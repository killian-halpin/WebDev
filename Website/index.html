About Cahir GAA

Cahir GAA was founded in 1885. It is a club in the South of Tipperary abpout twenty minutes away from Thurles. It's nickname is the slashers. The Colours of the club are green and white. Cahir wouldn't have a big pick of players with all the surrounding towns and villages having their own clubs so it is largely locally run. We are a dual Club. We field Junior hurling teams and a Senior Football team. Our only success to date was winning the 2003 Senior County Championship.

Our Success

The ladies hold most of the success in the club to date. They have many success storys in the last couple of years. These inlude All Ireland finals and many more.

Our Facilites

We have two pitches. Duneske and what we call the Park. They are not fantastic pitches and could do with being done up. I will tak more about the pitches later.
